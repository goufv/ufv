UFV > myClass
 

You've reached UFV's myClass site for Faculty and Student. At UFV, our Learning Management System (or LMS) is Blackboard Learn. The Student center is where you will find essential information and tutorials. The Faculty Centre is where instructors can access tutorials, request develomental shells, and request an evaluation of your Fully Online Course. There are tutorials for our web conferencing tool in myClass called Blackboard Collaborate and it is also available outside myClass by contacting Educational Technology Services (ETS). Another tool used in myClass is Kaltura, UFV's video management system. This tool allows us to upload audio and video files within a course and assignments and can be used outside of a course, in our Mediaspace (UFV's youtube environment).
