Explore On-Campus Living with Lauren Dallow

What’s it like to live on campus?

Are you planning to stay on campus at UFV?

Baker House, your home away from home, will provide you with so many opportunities to immerse yourself in campus life.

Meet friends, get involved in campus life, and enjoy the close proximity to on-campus dining and the UFV bookstore.

 

My one piece of advice to new students would be to get involved. Go to the events, make connections, and become a part of the community.

— Lauren Dallow
