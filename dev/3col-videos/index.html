What our students say

Even though everything is online right now, receiving help and connecting with other UFV faculty, staff and students electronically helps to keep our community intact.

My professor Claire Carolan was instrumental in making my first online course stress free. She was very clear with her instructions, provided supportive answers and posted her assignments into Blackboard in a timely manner.

UFV Teaching and Learning Centre helped me make a smooth transition for using my laptop for online courses. The Academic Success Centre and UFV Library resources were also very useful to me with doing my assignments.

Rachel Tait
Bachelor of Arts, English

The thing I liked most about doing online courses at UFV was the care of my instructors. Even though we as students are working on our own time and self-managing, our professors put in so much time and care into making sure we have what we need to be successful. They truly care about their students' success and are always available for extra help.

Take advantage of everything UFV has to offer! There are so many ways to get involved and if you can't find the opportunity you're looking for, I guarantee you UFV will support you in creating it.

Cambree Lovesy
Bachelor of Arts, Media and Communications

Drop-ins, workshops, and orientation events

The following sesssions are available for future and current students.

All students

Fall registration drop-in appointments

Daily

Call 604-864-4674 to book a same-day 30-min virtual drop-in appointment with an academic advisor to get assistance with fall registration or course planning.

Smart Start online orientation

August 2020, to be announced

A series of 4 modules to help you build your skills for academic, professional and personal success.

International students

One-on-one virtual drop-in appointments

Daily

Book an appointment with one of our international advisors and regulated immigration consultants. Choose from a variety of topics including study/work permits, registration and English upgrading support, accounts and fees, and others. Appointments can be booked daily from 8 am.

Immigration workshop

August 17, 4-5 pm

Get answers to your questions about permits and visas, inviting family members, immigration rules on academic standing, non-study, and change of program.

Missed an event?

Check the Discover your UFV video library to access recorded information sessions about registration, academic programs, services and support, and study permits.

Ask us today

Let us help keep you moving to the next step by answering any of your general UFV questions about admissions and academic programs, student life and events, or financial aid and registration.

The University of the Fraser Valley is situated on the unceded traditional territory of the Stó:lō peoples. The Stó:lō have an intrinsic relationship with what they refer to as S'olh Temexw (Our Sacred Land); therefore, we express our gratitude and respect for the honour of living and working in this territory.
