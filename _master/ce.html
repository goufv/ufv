Katie: Continuing to Build a Life for Her Family

Before registering at UFV, Katie worked as a hotel maid “making minimum wage as a young, Indigenous, single mom.” After completing the Legal Admin Assistant program, she was able to work in the field for a few years—and is now also completing the Paralegal program. "I never would have been able to work in this field if it weren’t for my education at UFV," she says. “I never thought I could be where I am today, but with a little bit of faith and a lot of hard work, I’m very successful in my life and my career—I was even able to purchase my own home for my kids— and it is all because I chose to further my education.”

Explore our Legal Administrative Assistant Certificate Program

Alison: Continuing Her Lifelong Learning

As a self-described “lifelong learner,” Alison signed up for, and completed her Public Relations Certificate—while also working towards her Bachelor's Degree. She is now working in the aerospace sector, providing media liaison support to her team. For Alison, program flexibility was one of the key reasons for her achievements. “The programs can be easily integrated into my life,” she notes, and have allowed her to successfully balance full-time work, personal and family commitments, alongside her education.

Explore our Public Relations Certificate Program

Jaspreet: Continuing Opportunities to Grow

For Jaspreet, an education in Human Resources Management has provided her with more than just training and certification. “The programs have given me opportunities to face obstacles and taught me how to celebrate the success of others,” she says. “UFV has provided me with the two most important qualities that I can possess: knowledge and confidence.” She now says her “forever goal” is to grow and learn, and to continue to accept new challenges in her career.
