What will your semester look like?

The university semester is different this year, but we've got you covered - this will be an amazing year.

UFV has an incredibly welcoming online community where you will find strength from faculty, staff and resources to support and guide you through your challenges and help build your confidence towards your academic goals.

Let your UFV online experience inspire and motivate you.

If this is your first year of university and you’re not sure if taking an online course online is for you, we encourage you to challenge yourself and try something different. UFV faculty work hard to make their online courses positive, interactive and engaging for their students through online learning tools and activities. You will have flexible opportunities to ask questions to your instructor and bond with students from around the world and build meaningful connections.
